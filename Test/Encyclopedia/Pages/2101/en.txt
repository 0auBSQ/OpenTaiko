"完走!激辛" Survival Hard: 
Harsher, stricter judging for Survival mode. Extra time is also capped. 



Super Hard Mode (Survival Super Hard): 
Enabled with F5 at music select. Only active when Survival Hard is selected.

The requirements for each criteria are raised significantly, amplifying
the difficulty even further. Penalties are increased. 
no bonus points are awarded.
